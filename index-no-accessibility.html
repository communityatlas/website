Community Atlas provides dynamic maps of relationships and resources, enabling commercial, civic, academic, and government organizations to connect and collaborate more effectively.

We map the diverse resources to which organisations connect, enabling an emerging network of collaborators.

Effective collaboration enables:

Stronger public legitimacy

Reduced implementation costs

Lower-risk project delivery

New strategic opportunities

The Community Atlas is a public resource designed to protect private information. It is based on four pillars.

Safety

We implement legal and operational systems to protect confidential information

Trust

We invest time to listen, learn and understand each organization’s priorities, concerns and constraints

Compatibility

We use a simple and transparent method to evaluate compatibility before recommending any collaborator

Alignment

We support processes that help to sustain alignment and ensure shared benefits for stakeholders

Initial milestone:

A validated proof of concept delivered within 12 months.

How we work

We work with commercial, civic, and public partners to co-develop the Community Atlas. By taking the time to listen, we learn and build trust across all stakeholders. The one-year milestone is the delivery of Community Atlas 1.0.
